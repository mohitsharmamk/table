About Us



JRK Industry

We are one of the professionally managed organizations engaged in manufacturing of a wide range of Modular Kitchen, Doors, Wardrobes, Sofas. These are made using
high quality raw material which is procured form reliable and certified sources. With constant innovation in our range of products, we have made an incredible mark in
the industry and have earned accolades form our clients spread all across the globe.

Over the last five decades, JRK Industry has managed to capture the attention of every citizen in this country. It started with a humble vision of manufacturing
electrical products of outstanding quality at a time when the market involving electrical switches or wiring devices was handled by the unorganized sector. The advent
of JRK Industry not only organized the sector, but also brought electrical accessories into mainstream commerce. As a brand, JRK signifies Trust, Safety and Reliability.
These qualities have helped JRK Industry carve a niche for itself in the electrical accessories market.

Our Mission & Vision

JRK Industry’s mission is to manufacture, innovative, eco-friendly, energy saving world-class products for making user’s life simpler, safer and comfortable.
